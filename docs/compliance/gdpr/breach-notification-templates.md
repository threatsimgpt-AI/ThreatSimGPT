**ThreatSimGPT Breach Notification Templates**

**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** January 20, 2026  
**Legal Basis:** GDPR Articles 33-34  
**Owner:** Compliance Team  

---

**Overview**

This document provides templates for data breach notifications required under GDPR. Use these templates to ensure timely, complete notifications to supervisory authorities and affected individuals.

---

**1. Supervisory Authority Notification (Article 33)**

**Timeline:** Within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach

**Section Template 1A: Initial Notification**

```
PERSONAL DATA BREACH NOTIFICATION
Pursuant to Article 33 of the General Data Protection Regulation

SECTION A: DATA CONTROLLER INFORMATION

1. Controller Name: ThreatSimGPT Project
2. Contact Person: [Name], [Title]
3. Email: privacy@threatsimgpt.io
4. Phone: [Phone Number]
5. Data Protection Officer: dpo@threatsimgpt.io

SECTION B: BREACH DETAILS

6. Date breach discovered: [YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM UTC]
7. Date breach occurred (if known): [YYYY-MM-DD or "Under investigation"]
8. Date of this notification: [YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM UTC]

9. Nature of the breach (select all that apply):
   [ ] Confidentiality breach (unauthorized disclosure)
   [ ] Integrity breach (unauthorized alteration)
   [ ] Availability breach (loss of access)

10. Description of the breach:
    [Provide a clear, factual description of what happened, how it was 
    discovered, and the circumstances surrounding the incident.]

SECTION C: DATA AFFECTED

11. Categories of personal data affected:
    [ ] Names
    [ ] Email addresses
    [ ] IP addresses
    [ ] Account credentials (hashed)
    [ ] Usage data
    [ ] Other: [Specify]

12. Categories of data subjects affected:
    [ ] Platform users
    [ ] Employees
    [ ] Other: [Specify]

13. Approximate number of data subjects affected:
    [ ] Exact count: [Number]
    [ ] Estimated range: [Number] to [Number]
    [ ] Unknown - investigation ongoing

14. Approximate number of records affected:
    [ ] Exact count: [Number]
    [ ] Estimated range: [Number] to [Number]
    [ ] Unknown - investigation ongoing

SECTION D: CONSEQUENCES

15. Likely consequences of the breach:
    [Describe potential impact on affected individuals, such as:
    - Risk of identity theft
    - Financial loss
    - Reputational damage
    - Loss of confidentiality
    - Other harm]

16. Risk assessment:
    [ ] No risk to individuals
    [ ] Low risk
    [ ] Risk to rights and freedoms
    [ ] High risk to rights and freedoms

SECTION E: MEASURES TAKEN

17. Measures taken to address the breach:
    [List containment and remediation actions, such as:
    - System isolation
    - Password resets
    - Security patches applied
    - Access revoked]

18. Measures taken to mitigate adverse effects:
    [List steps to reduce harm to affected individuals]

19. Measures planned but not yet implemented:
    [List additional planned actions with timelines]

SECTION F: COMMUNICATION TO DATA SUBJECTS

20. Have affected individuals been notified?
    [ ] Yes - Date: [YYYY-MM-DD]
    [ ] No - Planned date: [YYYY-MM-DD]
    [ ] No - Not required (risk assessment indicates no high risk)
    [ ] No - Notification would involve disproportionate effort

21. If not notified, explain why:
    [Provide justification]

SECTION G: ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

22. Is this a complete notification?
    [ ] Yes
    [ ] No - Additional information to follow

23. If incomplete, what information is pending?
    [List missing information and expected completion date]

24. Attachments:
    [ ] Incident report
    [ ] Technical analysis
    [ ] Risk assessment
    [ ] Other: [Specify]

DECLARATION

I confirm that the information provided is accurate to the best of my knowledge
and that this notification is made in accordance with Article 33 of the GDPR.

Name: ________________________
Title: ________________________
Date: ________________________
Signature: ____________________
```

**2. Data Subject Notification (Article 34)**

**Timeline:** Without undue delay when high risk to rights and freedoms

**Section Template 2A: Individual Notification Email**

```
Subject: Important Security Notice from ThreatSimGPT

Dear [User Name],

SECURITY INCIDENT AFFECTING YOUR ACCOUNT

We are writing to inform you of a security incident that may have affected
your personal data. We take the security of your information seriously and
want to provide you with the details and steps you can take.

WHAT HAPPENED

On [date], we discovered [brief, clear description of the incident]. 
[Explain how it was discovered and contained.]

WHAT DATA WAS INVOLVED

The following types of your personal data may have been affected:
- [Data type 1]
- [Data type 2]
- [Data type 3]

[If credentials were affected:]
Your password was stored in encrypted form, but as a precaution, we 
recommend changing your password.

WHAT WE ARE DOING

We have taken the following steps to address this incident:
- [Action 1]
- [Action 2]
- [Action 3]

We are also [describe ongoing measures].

WHAT YOU CAN DO

We recommend you take the following precautions:

1. Change your password: [Link to password reset]
2. Enable two-factor authentication: [Link to 2FA settings]
3. Monitor your account for unusual activity
4. [Any other specific recommendations]

[If applicable:]
Be cautious of phishing attempts. We will never ask for your password 
via email.

MORE INFORMATION

For more information about this incident:
- Visit our security notice page: [URL]
- Contact our privacy team: privacy@threatsimgpt.io
- Contact our DPO: dpo@threatsimgpt.io

We sincerely apologize for any concern this may cause. Protecting your 
data is our top priority, and we are committed to preventing incidents 
like this in the future.

Sincerely,

[Name]
[Title]
ThreatSimGPT Security Team

---
Data Protection Officer: dpo@threatsimgpt.io
You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.
```
